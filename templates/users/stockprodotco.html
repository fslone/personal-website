Still under construction (with a ways to go), StockPro.co is a stock market analytics platform for beginning investors. Mock portfolios, of course based on actual real-time price data, are the core of the platform and allow users to test their investing skills risk free. The Education Center provides a step-by-step guide to investing, and a points system gamifies the entire process of learning and rewards smart investing.

Some history: I invested my first few thousands in late 2008/ early 2009. Long story short, I made quite a bit of money. During that time, however, I cannot recall a single person supporting my decision to invest; I realized most people know very little about the stock market, and more often than not misconceptions prevail. After investigating numerous stock analytic and education sites, I was unable to find many resources for beginning investors. Even Stock Investing for Dummies was overly complicated and very opinionated. Thus, SP was born.

I hold a BS in Economics from the Georgia Institute of Technology, so I feel I have enough knowledge to be able to teach beginners. I hope to inspire and empower those who are too risk-adverse to participate in the market.
