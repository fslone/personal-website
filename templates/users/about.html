BIOGRAPHY
In high school I began developing software and small websites for various local organizations. Not certain that I wanted to pursue programming full-time, I moved to Atlanta to attend the Georgia Institute of Technology to pursue degrees in Accounting, Marketing and Internet Technology Management.

While at Tech I continued to develop software and offer hosting services under the One Side One name while also creating innovative marketing campaigns for small e-commerce sites like Brick-ANew. More and more opportunities began to roll in, and after graduating from Tech in 2011 I expanded my talents to larger companies, eventually working on campaigns for high profile clients like WebMD and Chick-Fil-A before coming to accept an offer for NOMi in 2013 that I still hold today.

When I'm not churning out code you can find me hiking on the Appalachian Trail, playing guitar, or in class working on a psychology degree at Oglethorpe University. Follow me on Instagram if you would like to see me doing stuff.
