BIOGRAPHY
I grew up in a quaint southern town called Valdosta, Georgia. In early high school I began designing and developing static websites for various organizations. Soon after graduating I made the jump to Atlanta to attend the Georgia Institute of Technology to pursue degrees in Economics and Aerospace Engineering.

In January 2013, I was approached by a high school friend to develop a social-media platform; I agreed, and thus began my steep plunge into application development. Since then, I have been lucky enough to work on some great projects and meet many awesome people. I have truly loved every minute of it.

Outside of code, I enjoy typical things with biking, music, and Sweetwater IPA topping the list. Follow me on Instagram if you would like to see me doing stuff. Like every pet owner ever, I think my dog is the cutest thing in the world.
