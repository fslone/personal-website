DinnerCrunch is a fresh take on recipe sharing applications. Chefs (on DC, everyone is a chef) enjoy a unique recipe uploader which will soon automatically calculate nutritional information, organizational tools to maintain cookbooks, and grocery lists to get you out of the supermarket faster. Head to dinnercrunch.com to check it out.

The brainchild of Will McCartney, I began working on DinnerCrunch in January of this year. Being the only developer on the team, I have been able to work faster than ever before. After a mere four months of developing from scratch, we are gearing up for marketing efforts and plan to create a strong community of users within a few months.

As with the majority of my projects, DinnerCrunch is built on Django with a MySQL database.

Also feel free to check out my DC profile and see what I've been cooking.
